About AAYPL

The All Africa Young Political Leaders (AAYPL) is a leading pan-African organization dedicated to uniting, empowering, and amplifying young political leaders across the continent. Our mission is to foster democratic values, ethical leadership, and policy influence through a strong and diverse community of youth changemakers.

What We Do

  • Organize summits, conferences, and leadership development programs
  • Facilitate policy dialogues and advocacy campaigns
  • Promote youth participation in democratic processes
  • Support youth-led initiatives and community projects
  • Provide mentorship and networking opportunities

Our History

  • Founded in 2017 by a coalition of passionate young leaders from 12 African countries
  • Now present in over 50 African nations and in the diaspora
  • Recognized by major continental and international organizations

Strategic Objectives

  • Empower youth with the skills and knowledge to lead
  • Advocate for youth-friendly policies at all levels
  • Facilitate cross-cultural exchanges and partnerships
  • Drive innovation in governance and civic engagement

Our Journey

2017: AAYPL is founded by a small group of youth activists in Lagos, Nigeria.
2019: First Pan-African Youth Democracy Summit held in Nairobi.
2021: Launch of the AAYPL Policy Innovation Lab.
2023: Membership reaches 10,000, with representation in every African country.
2024: AAYPL wins the Africa Rising Youth Award for Leadership.

Our Impact

  • More than 200 youth-led policy proposals adopted at national or local level
  • Hundreds of young leaders elected or appointed to public office
  • Thousands of lives improved through community initiatives
